Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our solutions

What is the typical return on investment for a solar system?

The ROI for solar systems typically ranges from 5-8 years, depending on your energy consumption, system size, local electricity rates, and available incentives. Our detailed proposal will include a customized ROI calculation for your specific situation.

How long do solar panels last?

Modern solar panels have a lifespan of 25-30 years, with manufacturers typically providing performance warranties guaranteeing at least 80% of rated output after 25 years. The inverters generally last 10-15 years and may need replacement during the system's lifetime.

What maintenance is required for a photovoltaic system?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dust and debris (2-4 times per year), occasional inspection of electrical components, and monitoring system performance are typically sufficient. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages to ensure optimal system performance throughout its lifespan.

Can I store excess energy for later use?

Yes, with battery storage systems, you can store excess energy generated during peak production periods for use during evenings or cloudy days. This is particularly beneficial for off-grid systems or in areas with frequent power outages or time-of-use electricity rates.

What financing options are available for renewable energy systems?

We offer various financing options including direct purchase, loans with favorable terms, leasing arrangements, and power purchase agreements (PPAs). Additionally, we help clients navigate available government incentives, tax credits, and subsidies to maximize financial benefits.
