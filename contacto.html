Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We are committed to responding to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Do you offer site visits to evaluate my property for installation?

Yes, we offer complimentary technical site visits to assess your property's conditions and design the most suitable solution for your needs. Our experts will evaluate factors such as orientation, shading, and available space to maximize energy production.

Do you offer financing options for photovoltaic systems?

Yes, we offer various financing options and can help you navigate available government incentives and subsidies. Our financial specialists will work with you to find the most advantageous solution for your investment.

How do I enroll in ITER Academy courses?

You can enroll through this contact form, by phone, or by visiting our facilities. Our education advisors will guide you through the entire process, from course selection to registration and payment options.

What information should I prepare before requesting a quote?

To provide you with the most accurate quote, it's helpful to have your recent electricity bills, property information (size, orientation), and specific goals (energy independence, bill reduction, etc.).
