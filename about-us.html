Our Mission & Vision

At ITER, we are committed to accelerating the global transition to sustainable energy through innovative technology solutions and education. Our mission is to provide accessible, efficient, and reliable renewable energy systems that empower individuals and organizations to reduce their carbon footprint while optimizing energy consumption.

Our vision is a world where clean energy is the standard, not the alternative. We strive to be at the forefront of renewable energy innovation, constantly pushing boundaries to make sustainable energy solutions more effective, affordable, and widely available.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

2010

Foundation

ITER was founded with a vision to make renewable energy accessible to everyone. Starting with a small team of three engineers, we began designing custom photovoltaic solutions for residential properties.

2013

Commercial Expansion

We expanded our services to commercial clients, completing our first large-scale installation for a manufacturing facility that reduced their energy costs by 35%.

2015

ITER Academy Launch

Recognizing the need for skilled professionals in the renewable energy sector, we established the ITER Academy to provide specialized training and certification programs.

2018

ISO 50001 Certification

We achieved ISO 50001 certification and began offering energy management consulting services to help organizations optimize their energy consumption and achieve international standards.

2020

International Partnerships

We formed strategic partnerships with global leaders in renewable energy technology, including Solar Energy International, expanding our reach and capabilities.

2023

500th Project Milestone

We celebrated our 500th successful project implementation, having installed over 10MW of photovoltaic capacity and trained more than 1,000 renewable energy professionals.

Today

Looking to the Future

We continue to innovate and expand our services, focusing on advanced energy storage solutions and smart grid integration to further enhance energy independence and efficiency.
